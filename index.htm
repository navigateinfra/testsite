Helping you navigate complex regulatory environments

The infrastructure industries are heavily influenced by policy and regulation. Strategy in these sectors must factor in current and future regulation, and the most successful companies will make it central to their plans.  Companies, Government and regulators alike will also be interested in achieving good regulation that moves the sector forward in the most efficient way. Navigate Infrastructure Consulting is an independent consultancy with the goal of helping clients in infrastructure industries to navigate complex regulation and policy situations to achieve optimal outcomes for your business and the sector as a whole. We can help you navigate: Policy and strategy development Economic and environmental regulation Market reform and regulatory change Long term planning Compliance with complex regulation, regulatory reporting and market codes Investment cases, value for money assessment and investment appraisal New ways of operating to address new challenges Our values are to: Give independent, honest advice about how you can improve your business and your plans Help you solve complex problems Be sensitive to your business environment, current and future culture Help you meet the long term challenges you face

About us

Nicky Fomes is Navigate Infrastructure Consulting’s Managing Director.  She has 18 years’ experience of working in regulated industries, principally the water, transport, and energy sectors. Nicky started her career working for an economic regulator (Ofwat), has headed a regulation team in a water company (Mid Kent Water) and in the utilities strategy team at PwC.  She founded Navigate Infrastructure Consulting in 2018.  Nicky has a PhD in Earth Sciences. Her clients include utilities companies, and other professional services firms as a subcontractor.  In previous roles, Nicky has worked for utilities, oil majors, mining companies, Government departments, economic regulators, environmental and standards regulators, consumer bodies, investors (and prospective investors), bondholders specialising in infrastructure, Government owned companies, utilities, PPP contractors, and the infrastructure supply chain. Navigate Infrastructure Consulting is part of a network of independent consultants who team together to solve complex problems using multiple skillsets.
